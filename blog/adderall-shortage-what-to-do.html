The Adderall Shortage Is Real — Here's What Your Prescriber Should Be Doing

IR vs XR. Pharmacy switching. Prior auth. If your provider isn't navigating this for you, you need a new provider.

March 16, 2026·5 min read·Dr. Ivy Jones, PMHNP-BC DNP

Your pharmacy called. Your Adderall IR 20mg is on backorder. Again. Your provider's office told you to try a different pharmacy. That's not a solution. That's a handoff.

The IR vs XR Decision

Amphetamine salts come in Immediate Release (IR) and Extended Release (XR). The shortage has affected IR significantly more than XR due to manufacturing concentration at specific facilities. A competent prescriber should be evaluating whether your clinical profile supports a formulation switch.

The medication shortage is a supply chain problem. Your provider's job is to solve the clinical problem, not to forward it to you.

What Active Management Looks Like

Proactive outreach when shortage data indicates your medication is at risk. Formulary review to identify therapeutically equivalent alternatives. Prior authorization submission for brand-name when generics are unavailable. CoverMyMeds PA check as a parallel step. Electronic prescribing to multiple pharmacies simultaneously to find available stock.

Same-Week Appointments. No Waitlist.

Telehealth psychiatric care in Texas and Washington. Medicaid accepted.
