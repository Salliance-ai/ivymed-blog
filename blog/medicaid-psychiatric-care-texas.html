Medicaid Covers Psychiatric Care in Texas — But Nobody Told You That

STAR, CHIP, and TMHP cover telehealth psychiatric visits. Most patients don't know. Most providers don't advertise it. We do.

March 19, 2026·5 min read·Dr. Ivy Jones, PMHNP-BC DNP

Your child has a CHIP card. Your household is on STAR. You've been told by three different clinics that they don't take Medicaid. You've started to believe that mental health care isn't available to you. It is.

What Texas Medicaid Actually Covers

Under TMHP, the following psychiatric services are covered: telehealth psychiatric evaluations, medication management visits, ADHD assessment and treatment, anxiety and depression management, and child and adolescent psychiatric services under CHIP.

Coverage exists. The gap is in providers willing to navigate the billing. We built the infrastructure to do exactly that.

Why Most Clinics Say No

Medicaid reimbursement rates are lower than commercial insurance. Billing Medicaid requires specific credentialing with TMHP. Many private practices make a business decision not to participate. IvyMed made the opposite decision — because the patients who need care most are often the ones the system has decided are too expensive to serve.
